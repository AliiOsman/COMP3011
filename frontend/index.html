Why This Feature Exists

No dataset exists for 2026 F1 because the season has not happened. Traditional data-driven methods cannot answer strategic questions about a future regulation set. This endpoint bridges that gap by combining real historical constructor Elo ratings from this database with AI reasoning about the known 2026 regulation changes.
