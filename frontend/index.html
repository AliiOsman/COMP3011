// llama 3.1 (groq) + historical elo data + 2026 regulation knowledge

Why This Feature Exists

No dataset exists for 2026 F1 because the season has not happened. Traditional data-driven approaches cannot answer strategic questions about a future regulation set. This endpoint bridges that gap — it fetches real historical Elo ratings from this database and sends them alongside the known 2026 regulation changes to an LLM, which reasons over both to produce regulation-aware strategic insight.
